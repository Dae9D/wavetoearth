love.

The song "사랑으로 (Love.)" by wave to earth is about the power of love to endure through tough times and bring people together. The lyrics describe the ups and downs of relationships, the challenges that come with pursuing one's dreams, and the importance of persevering through it all with the help of love. In the first verse, wave to earth mentions the errors on a printed page, which could symbolize the imperfections in life and relationships. They then describe the different forces in the world, comparing people to people and wind to wind. Despite these differences and competing desires, there's a sense of hope that individual yearnings can come together.
